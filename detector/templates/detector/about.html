LiveFace Detector

An intelligent system for detecting real-time presentation attacks (deepfakes) using AI.

Project Objective (Liveness Detection)

The primary goal of the LiveFace Detector is to prevent biometric spoofing (Anti-Spoofing) in identity verification systems. The system focuses on differentiating a genuine, live face presented to the camera from fraudulent attempts using non-live artifacts, such as:
1. Printed photos (paper masks). 2. Screen replay attacks (images/videos displayed on mobile phones or tablets).
This ensures high reliability in applications like attendance tracking or access control systems that rely on facial recognition, making it the first line of defense against visual identity fraud.
